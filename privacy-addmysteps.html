1. Introduction

Welcome to AddMySteps, operated by JarmSoft. We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy governs your use of the AddMySteps mobile application and explains how we collect, use, and share your information safely and compliantly.

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. This policy complies with Google Play guidelines, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other global data protection standards.

2. Health Data & Google Fit / Health Connect

AddMySteps is designed to write step data to your device's native health tracking systems (Google Fit / Health Connect). Because of the sensitive nature of health data, we adhere to strict privacy practices:

  • Local Processing: All health data you input is processed locally on your device and written directly to Health Connect or Google Fit API.
  • No Cloud Storage of Health Data: We do not transmit, upload, or store your health data, steps, or activity records on our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Explicit Consent: The app will explicitly request system-level permissions to read/write to Health Connect or Google Fit. You can revoke these permissions at any time via your device settings.

3. Data Collection and Usage

To provide core services, secure authentication, offer the PRO unlock, and run advertisements, we use selected third-party services.

A. Account and Authentication (Firebase Auth)

If you choose to create an account, we use Firebase Authentication. This collects:

  • Email address and basic profile information (if signing in via Google/Apple).
  • Authentication tokens to keep you securely logged in.

B. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to provide free access to this application. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information, such as:

  • Advertising Identifiers (e.g., Google Ad ID).
  • Device Information (IP address, operating system, network type).
  • Usage Data (ad interactions and application launch metrics).

C. Analytics and Crash Reporting (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to improve app stability and user experience. This collects:

  • Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics (e.g., which screens are visited most).

To manage PRO / Supporter purchases safely, we integrate RevenueCat. RevenueCat collects:

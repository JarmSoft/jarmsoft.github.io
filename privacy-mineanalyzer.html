1. Introduction

Welcome to MineAnalyzer, operated by JarmSoft. We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy governs your use of the MineAnalyzer mobile application on Android and iOS and explains how we collect, use, and share your information safely and compliantly.

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. This policy complies with Google Play and Apple App Store guidelines, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other global data protection standards.

2. Data Collection and Usage

MineAnalyzer is designed to function largely offline, processing all game calculations — mob stats, item values, enchantments, anvil costs, and portal math — locally on your device. However, to provide the service, offer the PRO unlock, and run advertisements, we use selected third-party services.

A. Data Stored Locally

  • App Preferences: Your selected theme (dark/light) and chosen language are stored locally on your device using Shared Preferences.
  • Game Calculations & Inputs: All data entered into the Mobs, Calculator, Anvil, and Portal tools is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded, retained, or processed on our servers.

B. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to provide free access to this application. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information, such as:

  • Advertising Identifiers (e.g., Google Ad ID, Apple IDFA).
  • Device Information (IP address, operating system, network type).
  • Usage Data (ad interactions and application launch metrics).

C. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

To manage MineAnalyzer Pro purchases safely, we integrate RevenueCat. RevenueCat collects:

  • Anonymous App User IDs generated upon installation.
  • Purchase history and entitlement status.

Note: All payment processing is handled natively by the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card details or billing information.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services. We highly recommend reviewing their privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services & AdMob
  • Apple App Store (iOS Only)
  • RevenueCat

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you are protected by the GDPR. Our app implements Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your explicit consent before showing personalized advertisements.

  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data. You have the right to object to processing and to data portability.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can manage and revoke your advertising consent at any time through your device's system settings or via the privacy options available in the app.

5. US State Privacy Compliance (CCPA / CalOPPA / CPRA)

If you are a resident of California or other applicable U.S. states, you are granted specific rights regarding your personal information.

  • Do Not Sell or Share My Info: We do not sell your personal data. We 'share' data strictly with our advertising partner (Google AdMob) to deliver personalized ads. You can opt-out at any time through your device's system settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android or "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS).

MineAnalyzer is a reference and calculation tool for Minecraft Java Edition and is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain EU jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will immediately delete it from our integrated systems.

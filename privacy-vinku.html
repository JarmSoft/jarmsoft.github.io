Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vinku, developed by JarmSoft. We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy governs your use of the Vinku — Mortgage Comparison Calculator mobile application on Android and iOS, and explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. This policy complies with Google Play Store and Apple App Store guidelines, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Spanish Organic Law on Data Protection (LOPDGDD), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable international data protection standards.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Vinku is designed to function primarily offline and locally. All mortgage calculations are performed entirely on your device. The financial data you enter (loan amounts, terms, interest rates, insurance costs) is never sent to our servers and is not accessible by JarmSoft. However, to provide free access, manage premium subscriptions, and retrieve public Euribor data, we use the following third-party services.

A. Data Stored Locally

  • User Preferences: Your selected theme (light/dark) and onboarding status are saved locally on your device using SharedPreferences. They are never transmitted to any server.
  • Mortgage Comparison Data: All data you enter in the comparison tool (loan capital, term, interest rate, bonuses, insurance, bank name, opening fees) is stored exclusively on your device and is not synced or shared with any third party.
  • Euribor Cache: The 12-month Euribor rate is downloaded from the European Central Bank (ECB) and cached locally for 24 hours to minimize mobile data usage. This data is publicly available and contains no personal information.

B. Advertising Data (Google AdMob) Free version only

We use Google AdMob to fund free access to the application. Users with an active subscription do not see any ads. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information, such as:

  • Advertising Identifiers (Google Ad ID / Apple IDFA).
  • Device Information (IP address, operating system, network type).
  • Usage Data (ad interactions and app launch metrics).

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, we implement Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your explicit consent before showing personalized ads, in compliance with the GDPR.

C. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions (RevenueCat) Premium

To securely manage annual subscriptions and one-time purchases, we integrate RevenueCat. This service collects:

  • Anonymous App User IDs generated upon installation.
  • Purchase and active subscription history.

Note: All payment processing is handled natively by the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. JarmSoft does not have access to, nor does it store, your credit card details or billing information.

D. Public External Data (European Central Bank API)

The application queries the ECB's public API to retrieve the 12-month Euribor rate. This request includes no personal data — only a public financial data point is requested. Your device's IP address may be visible to ECB servers during the request, as is standard with any internet connection.

E. Typography (Google Fonts)

The application uses the DM Sans typeface via Google Fonts. Google Fonts may receive a network request when the font is loaded, which may include your device's IP address. See the Google Fonts Privacy FAQ for more details.

3. Device Permissions

  • INTERNET: Required to fetch the current Euribor rate from the ECB, load advertisements (AdMob), and manage subscriptions (RevenueCat).
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Allows the app to check for an active internet connection before making network requests.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to send you local annual mortgage review reminders. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time from your device settings.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Allows the app to schedule your annual reminder at a precise date and time.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Allows scheduled reminders to be automatically restored if you restart your device.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID: Allows Google AdMob to use the device's advertising identifier to serve relevant ads (free version users only).

If you reside in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Spain, you are protected by the GDPR and the LOPDGDD. We apply the following measures:

  • Legal Basis for Processing: Advertising data is only processed based on your explicit consent, obtained through Google's UMP when you first launch the application.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You also have the right to data portability, the right to object to processing, and the right to restrict processing.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can manage and revoke your advertising consent at any time from your device's system settings (Android: "Ad Settings" → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"; iOS: "Privacy" → "Tracking").
  • Data Controller: JarmSoft — jarmsoft@gmail.com

If you are a resident of California or another U.S. state with applicable privacy legislation, you have specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising: You can disable ad personalization at any time from your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Vinku is a mortgage analysis and comparison tool intended exclusively for adults. It is not directed at children under the age of 16 (or under 13 under U.S. law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware of such a situation, please contact us at jarmsoft@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

Your trust matters to us. Your financial data is processed and stored exclusively on your device, with no transmission to external servers. For operations requiring internet access (advertising and purchase management), we rely on Google and RevenueCat, which implement industry-grade encryption and secure HTTPS connections.

9. Data Retention

Data stored locally (preferences, mortgage comparisons) remains on your device until you uninstall the application or delete it manually. Purchase history data held by RevenueCat is retained in accordance with their own retention policy. Advertising identifier data is managed according to Google AdMob's policy.

Welcome to BoxYield, operated by JarmSoft ("we", "us", or "our"). We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. This policy complies with Google Play and Apple App Store guidelines, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other global data protection standards.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Our application is designed to function largely offline and process financial calculations locally on your device. However, to provide the service securely, offer premium unlocks, and run advertisements, we use selected third-party services.

A. Data Stored Locally

  • App Preferences: Your selected themes (dark/light) and chosen language are stored locally on your device using Shared Preferences.
  • Calculations & Inputs: Yield, options, and strategy inputs are executed on your device and are not uploaded, kept, or processed on our servers.

B. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to provide free access to this application. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information, such as:

  • Advertising Identifiers (e.g., Google Ad ID, Apple IDFA).
  • Device Information (IP address, operating system, network type).
  • Usage Data (ad interactions and application launch metrics).

C. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

To manage "PRO" subscriptions and in-app purchases safely, we integrate RevenueCat. RevenueCat collects:

  • Anonymous App User IDs generated upon installation.
  • Purchase and subscription history.

Note: All payment processing is handled natively by the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card details or billing information.

3. Third-Party Services Links

  • Apple App Store (iOS Only)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you are protected by the GDPR. Our App specifically implements Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your explicit consent securely before showing personalized advertisements.

  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data. You have the right to object to processing and to data portability.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can manage and revoke your advertising consent at any time from the app's 'Privacy Preferences' icon in the settings menu.

5. US State Privacy Compliance (CCPA / CalOPPA / CPRA)

If you are a resident of California or other applicable U.S. states, you are granted specific rights regarding your personal information.

  • Do Not Sell or Share My Info: We do not sell your personal data. We 'share' data strictly with our advertising partner (Google AdMob) to deliver personalized ads. You can opt-out of personalized advertising at any time through your device’s system settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android or "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS).
